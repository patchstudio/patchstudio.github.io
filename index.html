About

Patch Studio was formed in 2016 by a small group of friends who like to do fun stuff. Currently, we're working on a game for PC and Playstation called Kiko that explores how children view death through the lens of a five year-old girl.

Join Patch

We're looking for team members! If you have an interest in joining Patch and working on our current project, Kiko, or would like to get to know more about how we operate as a company, feel free to email us with what you're interested in and your portfolio link at hello@patchstudio.com.

How'd you do that?

If you have any questions as to how we did something featured on Patch—whether it be a game technique, something on our website, or our stuff floating elsewhere on the web—drop us a line at hello@patchstudio.com and we'll lend a hand.
